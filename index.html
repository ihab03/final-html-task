Mr.Baker

Mr. Baker is a cozy spot in the heart of Palestine, famous for its delicious shawarma dishes With a friendly vibe, it's the perfect place to grab a bite with family and friends Each shawarma is packed with flavor, made from checken just right and wrapped in fresh bread You can customize it with tasty sauces and fresh veggies, making every bite a delight Whether you're a shawarma lover or not, Mr. Baker is all about good food and good times.
